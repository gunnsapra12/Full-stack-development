Ghar ka Khana — Ek Plate Mein Pura Sansaar

Blog Content:

“Kha liya beta?”
Sirf teen shabdon ka sawaal, lekin ismein chhupi hoti hai ek maa ki poori duniya.
Jab bhi maa phone karti hai, sabse pehle yahi poochti hai. Chahe main kitna bhi busy hoon, chahe call short ho, yeh ek sawaal hamesha hota hi hai.

🫓 Maa ke haathon ka swaad

Maa ka khana sirf khana nahi hota — woh ek emotion hota hai.
Us daal mein sirf masoor nahi hota, usmein hoti hai parwah.
Us sabzi mein sirf aloo-matar nahi, usmein hota hai pyaar.
Aur us roti mein sirf gehun ka aata nahi, usmein hota hai maa ka haath.

🏠 Hostel ki plate vs Ghar ki thali

Hostel mein khane ka kya hi kehna—kabhi namak zyada, kabhi sabzi kam.
Aur usi waqt yaad aata hai: maa ka woh haath se bana garam paratha, uske saath dahi, aur woh pyaar se bolna — “aur chahiye?”
Tab samajh aata hai: ghar ka khana sirf pet nahi, dil bhi bharta hai.

🍚 Ek Plate Yaadon Ki

Har kisi ka ek comfort food hota hai—mere liye woh hai rajma chawal.
Jaise hi plate mein aata hai, college ke stress, assignments, sab dissolve ho jaata hai.
Ek bite lete hi lagta hai jaise maa paas hi ho, pallu se haath pochti hui, muskurate hue keh rahi ho, “Aram se khao, aur lo na thoda aur.”
